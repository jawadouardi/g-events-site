Governance & Leadership

G EVENTS ASBL is committed to the highest standards of governance, transparency, and accountability in everything we do.

Our Governance Structure

As a Belgian ASBL (Association Sans But Lucratif), G EVENTS ASBL operates under a governance structure defined by Belgian law and our internal statutes. This structure ensures that our organization is managed responsibly, transparently, and in the best interest of the communities we serve.

Our governance framework is built on the principles of accountability, transparency, and participation. We believe that good governance is not just a legal requirement but a moral obligation to the people who support our work through donations, volunteering, and partnership. Every decision we make is guided by our mission and evaluated against the standard of whether it serves the public good.

G EVENTS ASBL is governed by an administrative board that provides strategic oversight and ensures that the organization remains true to its mission. The board is responsible for setting strategic direction, approving budgets and major expenditures, monitoring organizational performance, and ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.

Leadership Team

Our leadership team consists of dedicated individuals who bring a wealth of experience, expertise, and passion to G EVENTS ASBL. They serve without compensation, driven solely by their commitment to our mission of community enrichment. The board meets regularly to review organizational performance, discuss strategic priorities, and make decisions that advance our mission.

The day-to-day management of G EVENTS ASBL is handled by our administrator, who works closely with volunteers, partners, and stakeholders to plan and execute our events and programs. This lean operational structure ensures that the vast majority of our resources are directed toward our programs rather than administrative overhead.

Accountability to Our Stakeholders

We are accountable to multiple stakeholders: the communities we serve, our donors and supporters, our volunteers and partners, and the Belgian authorities that regulate non-profit organizations. We maintain open lines of communication with all of these groups and regularly report on our activities, finances, and impact.

Our commitment to accountability extends beyond legal compliance. We proactively seek feedback from event participants and community members, use this feedback to improve our programs, and share the results of our work with the public. We believe that transparency builds trust, and trust is the foundation of effective community service.

Governance Principles

How We Govern Ourselves

Our governance practices are guided by these core principles.

Transparency

We maintain open financial records and regularly communicate with stakeholders about our activities, finances, and impact. Our annual reports are available upon request.

Accountability

We are accountable to Belgian law, our members, and the communities we serve. We meet all regulatory requirements and undergo regular reviews of our activities and finances.

Integrity

We act with honesty and integrity in all our dealings. Our leaders serve without conflict of interest and recuse themselves from decisions where conflicts may exist.

Effectiveness

We measure and evaluate the impact of our programs to ensure that we are using resources effectively and achieving meaningful outcomes for the communities we serve.
