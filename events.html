Get Involved

Be Part of Our Events

There are many ways to participate in G EVENTS ASBL programs, whether as an attendee, volunteer, or partner.

👥

Attend

Many of our events are free or offered at reduced cost to ensure accessibility. Check our events calendar for upcoming opportunities to attend and experience our programs firsthand.

🙌

Volunteer

Our events rely on the dedication of volunteers who help with planning, logistics, hospitality, and more. Volunteering is a rewarding way to give back to your community and develop new skills.

🤝

Partner

We collaborate with organizations, businesses, and institutions to create events that serve our communities. If you are interested in partnering with us, we would love to hear from you.

Support Our Events

Your donations help us organize more events and make them accessible to everyone. Help us continue bringing communities together through culture, sports, and education.
