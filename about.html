About Us

Discover the story behind G EVENTS ASBL and our commitment to enriching communities through culture, sports, and education.

Our Story

G EVENTS ASBL was founded on July 9, 2009, in Belgium with a clear vision: to create meaningful cultural, sporting, and educational experiences that bring communities together and enrich the lives of people from all backgrounds. What began as a small initiative by passionate individuals has grown into a recognized non-profit organization that has organized over one hundred events and reached more than fifty thousand people across Belgium and beyond.

As a Belgian ASBL (Association Sans But Lucratif), we operate as a non-profit association under Belgian law. This legal structure reflects our fundamental commitment to serving the public good rather than pursuing commercial interests. Every euro we receive is directed toward our mission of community enrichment through events, programs, and initiatives that make a tangible difference in people's lives.

Over the years, we have built strong partnerships with local authorities, cultural institutions, educational organizations, and community groups. These collaborations have allowed us to expand our reach and impact, organizing events that range from intimate workshops and conferences to large-scale festivals and sporting competitions. Our work spans the fields of culture, arts, music, sports, education, and scientific exchange.

What Makes Us Different

At G EVENTS ASBL, we believe that culture and community are inseparable. Our events are not just about entertainment or competition; they are about building bridges between people, fostering understanding across cultural boundaries, and creating spaces where everyone feels welcome and valued. We are deeply committed to inclusivity, ensuring that our events are accessible to people of all ages, abilities, and backgrounds.

Our team consists of dedicated volunteers and professionals who share a passion for community service. Led by President Giovanni Fascianella and Administrator Wolfram Wambsganss, our governance structure ensures transparency, accountability, and effective stewardship of the resources entrusted to us by our donors and supporters.

Our Reach

Based in Schaarbeek, Brussels, G EVENTS ASBL serves communities throughout Belgium and has established connections with organizations across Europe. Our events take place in venues ranging from community centers and schools to concert halls and sports facilities, ensuring that we meet people where they are and bring our programs directly into the communities that benefit from them most.

We are proud of the impact we have made over the past fifteen years, but we know there is much more to be done. With the support of our donors, volunteers, and partners, we continue to expand our programs and reach new communities with events and initiatives that inspire, educate, and connect.
