Discover who we are, what drives us, and how we make a difference in communities across Belgium.

Who We Are

G EVENTS is a Belgian non-profit organization (ASBL - Association Sans But Lucratif) founded on July 9, 2009. Registered under enterprise number 0817.543.912, we are headquartered in Schaarbeek, Brussels.

Since our inception, we have been committed to enriching communities through the organization of cultural, sporting, and educational events. Our work brings people together, celebrates diversity, and creates meaningful experiences that resonate far beyond the events themselves.

As a non-profit, all our activities serve the public interest. We reinvest every resource back into our mission to create impactful events that inspire, connect, and uplift the communities we serve.

Our History

2009

Foundation

G EVENTS was founded by passionate individuals committed to bringing cultural and sporting events to Belgian communities. Registered as an ASBL, we began our journey with a clear vision and purpose.

2012

First Major Events

We organized our first large-scale cultural festivals, attracting hundreds of attendees and establishing our reputation as a reliable event organizer.

2016

Expansion

Our activities expanded to include sports competitions, academic conferences, and youth programs, reaching communities across the Brussels-Capital Region.

2020

Digital Innovation

We adapted to new challenges by incorporating virtual and hybrid event formats, ensuring our mission continued even during difficult times.

Today

Growing Impact

Today, G EVENTS continues to grow, organizing more events than ever and touching the lives of thousands of people each year through our diverse programs.

What We Do

🎭

Cultural Festivals

We organize exhibitions, art shows, and cultural festivals that celebrate the rich diversity of our communities and promote cross-cultural understanding.

🎵

Music & Concerts

From classical to contemporary, we host musical events that bring artists and audiences together for shared artistic experiences.

🏆

Sporting Events

We support and organize sports competitions that promote physical activity, healthy competition, and community spirit among participants.

📚

Conferences

Our conferences and seminars provide platforms for knowledge exchange, professional development, and intellectual discourse on important topics.

Academic Programs

Recognition Awards
