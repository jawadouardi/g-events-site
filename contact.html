Frequently Asked Questions

We welcome volunteers! Contact us using the form above or email us at markluismr@gmail.com with "Volunteer" in the subject line. We'll get back to you with information about upcoming volunteer opportunities.

We're always open to partnerships that align with our mission. Please reach out using the contact form or email us directly. We'll discuss potential collaboration opportunities that benefit both organizations and the communities we serve.

Thank you for your interest in supporting our work! As a non-profit, donations help us continue organizing impactful events. Contact us for information on how to make a donation. All donations are used exclusively for our mission.

Some of our events are free, while others may have a small entry fee to cover organizational costs. Check our Events page for details on specific events. We always strive to keep our events accessible and affordable.

We welcome event proposals that align with our mission. Please send your proposal to markluismr@gmail.com with a detailed description of your idea, including objectives, target audience, and proposed format.
