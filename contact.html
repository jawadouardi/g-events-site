FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about G EVENTS ASBL and our work.

G EVENTS ASBL is a Belgian non-profit association founded in 2009, dedicated to organizing cultural, sporting, and educational events that enrich communities across Belgium. We are registered as an ASBL under Belgian law with enterprise number 0817.543.912.

Many of our events are free and open to the public. Check our Events page for upcoming events and details on registration or admission. Some events may require advance registration due to capacity limits.

We welcome volunteers! Contact us through the form on this page or send an email to markluismr@gmail.com expressing your interest. We will get back to you with information about upcoming volunteer opportunities.

You can support our work by making a bank transfer to our Belgian IBAN account. Visit our Donate page for full details including our IBAN, BIC, and information on how your donation will be used.

Absolutely! We are always looking for partners who share our commitment to community enrichment. Whether you are a cultural institution, educational organization, business, or community group, we would love to explore how we can work together. Contact us to start the conversation.

As a Belgian ASBL, donations may be eligible for tax benefits under Belgian tax law. We recommend consulting with your tax advisor for specific guidance on the tax implications of your donation. Contact us if you need a donation receipt.
