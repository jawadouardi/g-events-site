Your Impact

We are committed to using every euro effectively. Here is how your donation is allocated.

Our Promise

100% to Our Mission

G EVENTS ASBL is a registered non-profit. We do not distribute profits. Every euro donated goes toward our mission of enriching communities through culture, sports, and education.

As a Belgian ASBL (Association Sans But Lucratif), G EVENTS ASBL is legally prohibited from distributing profits to members, directors, or any other individuals. This means that every donation we receive is used exclusively for our charitable purposes.

We maintain strict financial controls and regular oversight to ensure that funds are used responsibly and effectively. Our governance structure, led by President Giovanni Fascianella and Administrator Wolfram Wambsganss, provides strategic direction and accountability for all financial decisions.

We publish regular reports on our activities and finances, and we welcome questions from donors about how their contributions are being used. Transparency is a core value of our organization, and we believe that donors have the right to know exactly how their generosity is making a difference.

If you have any questions about donations or would like to discuss how your contribution can make the greatest impact, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are grateful for your support and committed to using it wisely.
