Our Mission

To enrich communities across Belgium and beyond through cultural, sporting, and educational events that inspire, connect, and uplift people from all walks of life.

Our Mission Statement

G EVENTS ASBL exists for one purpose: to make communities stronger, more connected, and more vibrant through the power of cultural, sporting, and educational events. We believe that when people come together to share experiences, whether through art, music, sport, or learning, they build the bonds that hold communities together and create lasting positive change.

Our mission is rooted in the conviction that every person deserves access to meaningful cultural and educational experiences, regardless of their background, age, or abilities. We work to remove barriers to participation and create events that are inclusive, welcoming, and enriching for everyone who attends.

As a Belgian non-profit association (ASBL), we are legally and ethically bound to use all of our resources in pursuit of this mission. We do not exist to generate profit; we exist to generate impact. Every decision we make, every event we organize, and every partnership we form is evaluated against a single question: does this serve our mission of community enrichment?

Why Our Mission Matters

In today's fast-paced world, genuine community connections are more important than ever. Studies consistently show that communities with strong cultural and social infrastructure are healthier, more resilient, and more cohesive. People who participate in cultural events report higher levels of well-being, stronger social connections, and a greater sense of belonging.

G EVENTS ASBL addresses these needs by creating events and programs that bring people together in meaningful ways. Whether it is a concert that celebrates cultural diversity, a sports competition that promotes teamwork and fair play, or a conference that advances knowledge and professional development, every event we organize contributes to the social fabric of our communities.

How We Fulfill Our Mission

We fulfill our mission through a diverse portfolio of events and programs spanning culture, arts, music, sports, education, and community development. Our approach is collaborative, working closely with local authorities, cultural institutions, educational organizations, and community groups to ensure that our events meet the real needs of the communities we serve.

We also invest in scholarships and awards that recognize outstanding achievements and encourage excellence. These programs provide tangible support to individuals who demonstrate exceptional talent, dedication, or service to their communities, helping them to further their development and contribute even more to society.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Us

These core values shape everything we do at G EVENTS ASBL, from the events we organize to the partnerships we form.

01

Inclusivity

We believe that cultural and educational experiences should be accessible to everyone. We actively work to remove barriers to participation and create events that welcome people of all ages, abilities, backgrounds, and identities.

02

Excellence

We are committed to organizing events of the highest quality. From planning to execution, we strive for excellence in every detail, ensuring that our participants have meaningful and memorable experiences.

03

Community

Community is at the heart of everything we do. We believe in the power of shared experiences to strengthen social bonds, foster understanding, and create a sense of belonging among diverse groups of people.

04

Transparency

We operate with complete openness and accountability. Our finances, governance, and decision-making processes are transparent, and we regularly communicate with our supporters about how their contributions are being used.

05

Sustainability

We are committed to organizing events in an environmentally and socially responsible manner. We consider the long-term impact of our activities and strive to create lasting positive change in the communities we serve.

06

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnership. By working together with other organizations, institutions, and community groups, we can achieve far more than we could alone and create events that truly reflect the needs and aspirations of our communities.

Strategic Goals

Where We Are Headed

Our strategic goals guide our work and ensure that we remain focused on maximizing our impact.

Expand Cultural Programming

We aim to increase the number and variety of cultural events we organize, reaching new communities and introducing fresh programming that reflects the evolving interests and needs of the people we serve.

Strengthen Youth Programs

We are committed to developing new programs specifically designed for young people, providing them with opportunities to develop skills, gain experience, and participate actively in cultural and community life.

Deepen Community Partnerships

We seek to build stronger and more diverse partnerships with local organizations, cultural institutions, and educational bodies to enhance the quality and reach of our events and programs.

Increase Accessibility

We are dedicated to making our events more accessible to people with disabilities, limited mobility, and those facing financial barriers, ensuring that no one is excluded from participating in our programs.

Promote Sustainability

We are working to minimize the environmental impact of our events by adopting sustainable practices in event planning, logistics, and operations, contributing to a healthier planet for future generations.

Enhance Financial Sustainability

We aim to diversify our funding sources and build a sustainable financial base that allows us to continue and expand our programs for years to come, reducing dependence on any single funding source.
